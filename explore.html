North | Amazônia

Embark on a soul-stirring expedition to the heart of the Amazon Rainforest with our exclusive 4-day itinerary. Begin your journey in Manaus, the vibrant gateway to the world's largest tropical rainforest, where lush greenery and exotic wildlife beckon. This curated experience invites you to unravel the mysteries of the Amazon, forging a deep connection with nature in every moment.

Join us on this extraordinary adventure where each day in the Amazon Rainforest is a revelation, promising a deeper understanding of the delicate balance between humanity and the natural world.

Northeast | Fernando de Noronha

Embark on an unforgettable journey to the pristine archipelago of Fernando de Noronha, where turquoise waters meet golden sands, and nature unfolds its breathtaking wonders. Our carefully crafted 4-day itinerary invites you to explore the natural beauty, marine treasures, and cultural richness of this Brazilian gem. From snorkeling in the crystalline waters of Baía do Sancho to encountering sea turtles at Projeto Tamar, each day promises a new adventure. Immerse yourself in the charm of Vila dos Remédios and witness mesmerizing sunsets that paint the sky in hues of orange and pink. Fernando de Noronha is not just a destination; it's a sanctuary where every moment is a celebration of nature's wonders. Join us on this journey, where paradise becomes a reality.

South | Gramado

Nestled in the heart of the Brazilian highlands, Gramado invites you to a magical retreat where the charm of a European-inspired town meets the breathtaking landscapes of the Serra Gaúcha. Our 3-day itinerary unfolds a tapestry of experiences, from exploring the cobblestone streets adorned with Bavarian architecture to savoring delectable chocolates and indulging in the warmth of local hospitality. Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Mini Mundo, witness the splendor of the Cascata do Caracol waterfall, and unwind at the stunning Lake Negro. As night falls, experience the wonder of the Gramado Natal Luz, a festival of lights that transforms the town into a fairy-tale wonderland. Join us for an unforgettable escape to Gramado, where every moment is infused with magic.
